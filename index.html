Summary

Scenario 2 is slightly more energy efficient and cheaper overall. Here's why:

  • The second boil only reheats pre-boiled water that remains at 67°C (summer conditions).
  • There’s no need to reheat cold water the second time, saving energy.
  • Total energy savings compared to Scenario 1: ~0.0096 kWh (~0.14 cents).

However, Scenario 1 avoids heating excess water, which may be more efficient in other seasons or if water is not reused.

Ultimately, Scenario 2 is more cost-effective in this specific case.
